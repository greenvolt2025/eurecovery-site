Українською

Mission

Ensuring Ukraine's energy, scientific, and digital integration into the EU through transnational cooperation, research, and implementation of innovation programs.

Program for Ensuring Energy Security of Ukraine and the EU

  • Conducting scientific and applied research in energy, sustainable development, and digital innovation;
  • Institutional support for building energy security at regional and national levels;
  • Participation in international research consortia, EU programs, and sustainable development initiatives;
  • Creation of analytical products, expert conclusions, and policy recommendations in energy policy.

International Cooperation

The Institute cooperates with EU partners, international donors, universities, R&D organizations, civil society, and open science platforms.
