  • Грантові програми ЄС: Horizon Europe, Digital Europe, LIFE.
  • Післявоєнне відновлення: розробка проєктів реконструкції критичної інфраструктури України.

Institute Programme

The Private Research Institution “International Institute for the Recovery and Development of Ukraine” is an independent organisation focused on interdisciplinary research, energy security and post‑war recovery.

Main Activities

  • Energy security & VPP: development of virtual power plants, flexibility trading via APIs.
  • Scientific research & R&D: collaboration with universities and technical labs.
  • EU Grant Programmes: Horizon Europe, Digital Europe, LIFE.
  • Post‑war reconstruction: designing projects to rebuild Ukraine’s critical infrastructure.
