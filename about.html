About Us

The International Institute for Development and Recovery of Ukraine is a private scientific institution dedicated to research in energy, IT, and law, funded by grants. Our mission is to ensure Ukraine's energy, scientific, and digital integration into the European Union through transnational cooperation, research, and innovative programs. Our team includes leading experts from the Academy of Legal Sciences of Ukraine, the National Law Academy of Ukraine named after Yaroslav the Wise, KPI, and Lviv Polytechnic.
