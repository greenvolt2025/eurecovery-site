Programs

Our institute implements the Energy Security Program for Ukraine and the EU, which includes: conducting scientific and applied research in the fields of energy, sustainable development, and digital innovations; providing institutional support for building energy security at the regional and national levels; participating in international research consortia, EU programs, and sustainable development initiatives; creating analytical products, expert opinions, and recommendations in the field of energy policy.
